Are you and your cat goin wild for some straight JUNK? Try blasting off with this bite-sized cheesy bullet

Ingredients

  • cheese
  • slop
  • tomato
  • onion
  • salt & peppa
  • extra virgin olive oil
  • garlic
  • quality bread/ butter

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Combine ricotta cheese, 1/2 of the mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese and Italian seasoning.
  3. Cook slop in a skillet until the time is right. Stir in marinara sauce.
  4. Carefully crisp up some bread, the right way
  5. Spread 1/6 of the cheese mixture evenly on each bread; cover with slop mixture. Top with remaining mozzarella cheese.
  6. Bake in the inferno until cheese is melted and bubbly, 10 to 15 minutes.
